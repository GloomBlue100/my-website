Welcome to Bean & Brew

Join us for weekly live performances, creative workshops, tasting sessions, and special themed nights. Whether you’re here to unwind or discover something new, our events add a little extra flavour to your visit.

Live Music Night

Join us every Friday for acoustic performances from local artists. Relax, unwind, and enjoy great music with your favourite brew.

Art & Crafts Night

A relaxed creative evening where customers can sketch, paint, or doodle while enjoying a warm drink. Materials provided, or bring your own. No experience needed — just good vibes.

Coffee Tasting Workshop

Learn the art of tasting coffee like a pro. Our baristas guide you through different beans, brewing methods, and flavour notes. Perfect for beginners and enthusiasts alike

Book Club

A monthly gathering for book lovers. Enjoy a cosy discussion, share recommendations, and connect with fellow readers over a cup of your favourite brew.
