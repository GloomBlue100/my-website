Welcome to Bean & Brew

From rich, small‑batch coffees to freshly prepared meals, our menu is crafted with simple, honest ingredients. Enjoy handcrafted drinks, homemade bakes, and seasonal dishes inspired by local produce.

Latte

A smooth blend of rich espresso and steamed milk, topped with a light layer of foam. Creamy, comforting, and perfect for slow mornings or relaxed afternoons.

Cappuccino

A bold espresso base balanced with equal parts steamed milk and velvety foam. Light, airy, and full of flavour — a classic café favourite.

Hot Chocolate

A cosy cup of silky, indulgent chocolate made with steamed milk. Sweet, warming, and topped with a gentle froth — the ultimate comfort drink.

Tea

A freshly brewed cup of premium loose‑leaf tea. Simple, soothing, and timeless — perfect on its own or paired with a sweet treat.
